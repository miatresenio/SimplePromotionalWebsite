Learn more about PRADA

Founded in 1913 in Milan, Prada has become one of the most influential fashion houses worldwide, known for its sophisticated and innovative designs. Over the years, Prada has expanded its offerings beyond clothing, delving into accessories, fragrances, and more.

Prada's collections are characterized by their fusion of classic and avant-garde elements, making the brand a symbol of luxury and contemporary style. From the iconic Galleria bag to the latest runway collections, Prada continues to set trends and redefine fashion.

Our Philosophy

At Prada, we believe in pushing the boundaries of fashion while staying true to our heritage. Our designs are a blend of tradition and innovation, resulting in timeless pieces that transcend trends.

Sustainability

We are committed to sustainable practices in every aspect of our business. From sourcing materials to our production processes, we aim to minimize our environmental impact and contribute to a better future.

Global Presence

With boutiques in major cities across the world, Prada has established itself as a global fashion leader. Our stores are designed to offer an immersive shopping experience, reflecting the brand's unique aesthetic.
